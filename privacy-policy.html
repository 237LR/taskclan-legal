Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to HomeHero. This Privacy Policy explains how Karol Jaworski ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use the HomeHero mobile application (the "App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect the following types of personal information when you use the App:

  • Account Information: Name, email address (optional for authentication), and avatar preference
  • Authentication Data: When using Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In, we receive authentication tokens and basic profile information
  • Usage Data: Task completion data, experience points (XP), streak data, rewards, and progress metrics
  • Family Group Data: Family member profiles, family group associations, and shared task data
  • Subscription Information: Subscription status and purchase history (processed through RevenueCat and Apple)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Analytics: App usage patterns, feature interactions, and crash reports
  • Local Storage: Data stored locally on your device for offline functionality

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide, maintain, and improve the App's functionality
  • Cloud Sync: To synchronize your data across devices when cloud sync is enabled
  • Family Sharing: To enable family group features and multi-user functionality
  • Subscription Management: To process and manage your subscription and payments
  • Communication: To send you service-related notifications and respond to your inquiries
  • Analytics: To analyze usage patterns and improve the App's features
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and fraudulent activity

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage (Offline Mode)

When using the App in offline/local-only mode, all data is stored exclusively on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Cloud Storage (Online Mode)

When cloud sync is enabled, your data is stored and processed using Supabase, our cloud database provider. Supabase implements industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest.

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure authentication protocols (OAuth 2.0)
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication requirements

Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

Supabase

Used for cloud database and authentication services. Data is stored on Supabase servers. View Supabase's privacy policy at: https://supabase.com/privacy

RevenueCat

Used for subscription management and in-app purchase processing. RevenueCat collects purchase information and subscription status. View RevenueCat's privacy policy at: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Google Sign-In

When you use Google Sign-In, Google's privacy policy applies to the authentication process. View Google's privacy policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Apple Sign-In

When you use Apple Sign-In, Apple's privacy policy applies to the authentication process. View Apple's privacy policy at: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

HomeHero is designed for family use and may be used by children between the ages of 6 and 16 under parental supervision. We are committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Parental Consent

Parents or legal guardians must create and manage family accounts. Children's profiles are created and managed by parents within the family group.

Children's Data Collection

For child users within a family group, we collect only:

  • Name (first name only, as entered by parent)
  • Avatar preference
  • Task completion data
  • XP, streak, and reward data

Parental Controls

Parents have full control over their children's data and can:

  • Review their children's information
  • Request deletion of their children's data
  • Refuse to permit further collection of their children's information
  • Remove children from the family group

No Direct Marketing to Children

We do not knowingly market to or collect personal information from children for marketing purposes.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With Supabase, RevenueCat, and authentication providers as necessary to operate the App
  • Family Members: Within your family group, certain data (task completion, progress) is shared with other family members
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (you will be notified)
  • Safety: To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or others

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. Retention periods:

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
  • Backup Data: May persist in backups for up to 90 days
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Portability

You can access your personal information through the App's profile settings. You may request a copy of your data by contacting us.

Correction

You can update your personal information directly in the App's settings.

Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and personal information by contacting us at karoljaworsky@gmail.com. Note that some information may be retained in backups for a limited time.

Opt-Out of Cloud Sync

You can use the App in offline/local-only mode, which prevents data from being transmitted to our servers.

Subscription Management

You can manage or cancel your subscription through your Apple App Store account settings.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using the App, you consent to such transfers. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Right to request deletion of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access to your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Significant changes will be notified through the App or via email. Your continued use of the App after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
