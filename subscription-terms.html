Free Trial

HomeHero offers a 3-day free trial for first-time subscribers to Monthly and Yearly plans.

Trial Terms

  • Duration: 3 days from the moment you subscribe
  • Eligibility: Available only to new subscribers who have never subscribed to HomeHero Premium
  • Full Access: During the trial, you have complete access to all premium features
  • Cancellation: You can cancel anytime during the trial period without charge
  • Auto-Renewal: If you do not cancel before the trial ends, you will be automatically charged and your paid subscription will begin
  • One Trial Per User: The free trial is limited to one per Apple ID

Trial Start and End

Your trial begins immediately when you subscribe. The exact end time is displayed in the subscription confirmation. To avoid charges, you must cancel at least 24 hours before the trial ends.

Auto-Renewal

All recurring subscriptions (Monthly and Yearly) automatically renew unless you cancel before the renewal date.

How Auto-Renewal Works

  • Automatic Billing: Your subscription will automatically renew at the end of each billing period
  • Same Price: You will be charged the then-current subscription price (prices may change with notice)
  • Apple Account: Charges are processed through your Apple ID account
  • 24-Hour Window: Your account will be charged within 24 hours before the current period ends
  • Renewal Date: The renewal date is based on your original subscription date

Managing Auto-Renewal

You can turn off auto-renewal at any time through your Apple ID account settings. Auto-renewal must be disabled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period to avoid being charged for the next period.

Lifetime Terms

  • Non-Transferable: Cannot be transferred to another Apple ID

Cancellation Policy

How to Cancel

To cancel your subscription:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iOS device
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select HomeHero
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

Alternatively, you can manage subscriptions through the App Store app or iTunes on your computer.

Cancellation Timing

  • During Trial: Cancel anytime to avoid being charged
  • Paid Subscription: Cancel at least 24 hours before renewal to avoid next billing cycle
  • Access Continues: You retain access to premium features until the end of the current billing period
  • No Partial Refunds: Cancellation does not provide a refund for the current period

After Cancellation

When your subscription ends:

  • You will lose access to premium features
  • Your data will be retained for 90 days in case you resubscribe
  • You can continue using the free version of HomeHero
  • Family members beyond the free limit will need to be removed

Refunds are granted at Apple's sole discretion. Common reasons for refund requests include:
